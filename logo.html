SubhanSound

Ottawa based radio show found in 2012, They play music you will never hear on local radios as well as provide a platform for local artists and creatives to showcase their work.

“Dope sound, and Being Yourself!”

Client

My client wanted to incorprate the name, "Subhan Sound" and combine it with the identity of the brand, "Dope sound, and Being Yourself", to create a logo that would represent a mix of culture, passion, and music in one image.
