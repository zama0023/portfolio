Subhan Sound

Type: Logo Design

Tools: Adobe Illustrator

Client

SubhanSound is an Ottawa based radio show founded in 2012. They play music you will never hear on local radios as well as provide a platform for local artists and creatives to showcase their work.

Challenge

The challenge was to incorprate the name, "Subhan Sound" and combine it with the identity of the brand, "Dope sound, and Being Yourself", to create a logo that would represent a mix of culture, passion, and music in one image.
