SubhanSound

Subhan Sound is an Ottawa-based radio show founded in 2012. They play music you will never hear on local radio. They provide a platform for local artists and creatives to showcase their work.

“Dope Sound, and Being Yourself!”

Client

My client wanted to incorporate the name "Subhan Sound". To combine it with the identity of their brand, "Dope sound, and Being Yourself". To create a logo that would represent a mix of culture, passion, and music in one image.
