“There's an idea in each story and it's my mission to bring these ideas to life, through design.”

Mehrara Zamani is a Graphic Designer, and photographer based in Canada. She has her degree in Graphic Design from Algonquin College. Mehrara loves exploring visual concepts, that's what sparks her drive to create. She gets most of her inspiration from fashion, movies, and music.

When she isn't creating, Mehrara is usually travelling, snowboarding, painting, or playing with her 2 cats.
