A short story of where it all began

When I was 3 I upgraded the living room of our home into my own gallery by taking all my pencil crayons and making huge murals on the walls. To my parents it was a mess, but I call it abstract. Even though my parents weren't pleased it didn't stop me from doing what I enjoyed, which was art!

Now thats settled...
lets move on to why I do what I do

I love exploring, that's why I chose graphic design. I simply enjoy finding new creative ways to communicate designs.

To me being a designer means I get to Build, Create, and Explore. If you're not learning new things and stepping out of your comfort zone you're not growing!
