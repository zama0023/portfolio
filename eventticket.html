BHM Gala

Tickets created for the Black History Month Gala held at the University of Ottawa. American Actress and singer/song writer Keke Palmer was the main guest speaker for the event.

Client

My client wanted to incorprate the name, "Subhan Sound" and combine it with the identity of their brand, "Dope sound, and Being Yourself", to create a logo that would represent a mix of culture, passion, and music in one image.
